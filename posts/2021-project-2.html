2021 Project #2

As part one of the fantastic challenges found on Frontend Mentor (opens new window), I have created a digital version of Rock, Paper, Scissors, Lizard, Spock. If you are unfamiliar with this improvement on the classic game of Rock, Paper, Scissors, watch as Sheldon from the Big Bang Theory explains the rules of the game.

As for my digital version, I had fun making it. Getting the buttons in a pentagram was a little tricky, and I used the Vue to speed up development. All in all, I think it turned out okay for just a couple of hours of work.
